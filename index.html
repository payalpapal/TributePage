The Real 'Ratan' of India


Ratan Naval Tata(Ratan Tata 28 December 1937) is an Indian industrialist, philanthropist and a former chairman of tata Sons.He was the chairman from October 2016 through February 2017, and continue to head its charitable trusts. He is the recipient of two civilian honour, and the padma Vibhushan(2008), the second highest civilian honour, and the Padma Bhushan(2000), the third highest honour

    Honour and Awards

  • The Padma Vibhushan
  • The Padma Bhushan
  • Commander of the Legion of Honour
  • Honory Doctor in Engineering
  • Honory Doctor of Literature
